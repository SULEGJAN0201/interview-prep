## üéØ Tech Lead vs. Senior Engineer

Understanding the difference is key:

| Aspect | Senior Engineer | Tech Lead |
|--------|----------------|-----------|
| **Primary Focus** | Technical excellence | Team success through technical leadership |
| **Success Metric** | Quality of your code | Team's velocity and growth |
| **Decisions** | How to implement | What to build and why |
| **Communication** | With team | With team, stakeholders, and leadership |
| **Growth** | Your skills | Team's skills and capabilities |
| **Time Allocation** | 90% coding, 10% other | 50% coding, 30% leadership, 20% strategy |

**Interview Focus Shift**:
- Senior Engineer: "Can you solve hard problems?"
- Tech Lead: "Can you lead a team to solve hard problems?"

## üéØ Quick Tips for Tech Lead Interview Day

### Before the Interview

1. **Prepare Your Story Bank**:
   - 3 mentoring/coaching stories
   - 2 conflict resolution examples
   - 3 architecture decision stories
   - 2 influence without authority examples
   - 1-2 failure/learning stories

2. **Review Your Projects**:
   - Quantify your impact (improved by X%, reduced by Y)
   - Understand team dynamics and growth
   - Know the trade-offs you made and why

3. **Refresh Technical Fundamentals**:
   - You'll still get technical questions
   - Be ready to discuss trade-offs, not just solutions
   - Focus on "why" over "how"

### During the Interview

#### For Behavioral Questions:
- Use STAR method religiously
- Focus on YOUR actions and decisions
- Quantify results whenever possible
- Show vulnerability (discuss learnings from failures)
- Balance technical and people stories

#### For Technical Questions:
- Discuss trade-offs explicitly
- Ask clarifying questions about scale, constraints
- Think out loud about your decision process
- Show how you'd involve the team
- Consider operational implications

#### For System Design:
- Start with requirements and constraints
- Discuss multiple approaches and trade-offs
- Justify decisions with data/reasoning
- Show how you'd validate the design
- Mention how you'd document it (ADRs)

### Questions to Ask Them

**About the Team**:
- "What does success look like for this role in the first 6 months?"
- "Tell me about the team I'd be leading - size, experience levels?"
- "What are the biggest challenges the team is currently facing?"

**About Leadership**:
- "How does the company support tech leads in developing leadership skills?"
- "What's the balance between coding and leadership in this role?"
- "How much autonomy do tech leads have in technical decisions?"

**About Culture**:
- "How does the team handle technical disagreements?"
- "What does the code review culture look like?"
- "How do you measure engineering success?"
